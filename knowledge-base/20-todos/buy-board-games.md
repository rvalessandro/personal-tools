- **Players**: 4-8+ (best with 6-8)

- **Players**: 2-6

- **Great for**: Mixed age groups

#### 4. **Love Letter**
- **Players**: 2-4 (up to 6 with expansions)
- **Type**: Deduction, hand management
- **Why**: Tiny box, huge fun, plays in 5-10 minutes
- **Perfect for**: Travel, quick sessions

### üß† Medium Complexity (30-60 min)

#### 5. **Ticket to Ride** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê
- **Players**: 2-5
- **Type**: Route building, set collection
- **Why**: Gateway game to modern board gaming, beautiful components
- **Versions**: Europe (best for first-timers), USA, Asia

#### 6. **Splendor**
- **Players**: 2-4
- **Type**: Engine building, resource management
- **Why**: Elegant, tactile (poker chips), quick turns
- **Great for**: People who like optimization puzzles

#### 7. **Azul**
- **Players**: 2-4
- **Type**: Abstract strategy, pattern building
- **Why**: Gorgeous components, simple but deep
- **Awards**: Spiel des Jahres 2018 (Game of the Year)

#### 8. **7 Wonders**
- **Players**: 2-7 (best with 4-6)
- **Type**: Card drafting, civilization building
- **Why**: Plays quickly even with 7 players, lots of strategy
- **Expansion**: 7 Wonders Duel (excellent 2-player version)
